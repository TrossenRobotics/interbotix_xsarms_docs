========================
Interbotix X-Series Arms
========================

.. image:: images/xsarm_family.png
    :width: 80%
    :align: center

Interbotix ROS robotic arms are the perfect addition to any research facility, university classroom or laboratory.
Featuring the DYNAMIXEL X-Series smart servos, the line offers 4, 5 and 6 degree of freedom platforms.
All of which are controlled by the same central ROS code repository.
All of the X-Series arms are supported with tutorials, videos and software demos including MoveIt and Gazebo simulation, as well as computer vision applications such as pick and place.
